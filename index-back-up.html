Research Focus

Post-fire forest resilience through collaborative monitoring and geospatial analysis

Reforestation Effectiveness Monitoring (REM)

Leading a collaborative project with natural resource organizations to establish standardized protocols for monitoring reforestation success in post-fire landscapes. Developed experimental design framework and completed first-year results analysis.

  • Multi-agency collaboration protocol established
  • Standardized monitoring methodology developed
  • First-year effectiveness results documented
  • Literature review and best practices compiled

Geospatial Analysis of Forest Resilience

Integrating remote sensing data with field measurements to understand spatial patterns of forest recovery and identify factors that enhance post-fire resilience in Sierra Nevada ecosystems.

  • Satellite data analysis (Landsat, Sentinel)
  • NDVI and vegetation index monitoring
  • Spatial pattern analysis of recovery success
  • Predictive modeling for reforestation planning
